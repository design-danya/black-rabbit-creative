Project Overview

Rooted in Pleasure

This project was developed as my Master of Fine Arts thesis in Media Design, exploring how thoughtful branding can reshape the visual language of the adult wellness industry.

Adult product brands typically fall into two visual extremes: sterile, clinical aesthetics or overtly explicit imagery. This thesis explored a third direction — a brand identity that celebrates craftsmanship, humor, nature, and body positivity while maintaining a refined retail presence.

The concept brand, Sequoia Sex Toys, is imagined as a socially conscious wooden toy company rooted in sustainable materials, artisan production, and open conversations around pleasure and intimacy.

The final outcome is a complete brand ecosystem designed to function across retail packaging, digital commerce, and lifestyle merchandise.

⸻

Research & Strategy

The thesis began with research into how branding shapes consumer perception in industries historically associated with stigma or discomfort.

Key observations included:

• Many adult brands rely on clinical minimalism, which can feel cold or impersonal.
• Others lean into shock value and explicit visuals, limiting mainstream appeal.
• Few brands balance playfulness, sophistication, and warmth.

Sequoia was designed to occupy this middle ground.

The brand positioning is built around three principles:

Nature – grounding the brand in natural materials and botanical symbolism
Craft – highlighting the handmade quality of wooden products
Play – embracing humor and storytelling to normalize conversations about pleasure

Logo Design

The Jackalope Identity

At the center of the brand identity is a mirrored jackalope crest — a mythical creature chosen to represent Sequoia’s playful and slightly irreverent spirit.

Two jackalopes face one another in symmetrical formation, their antlers interlocking to form a unified crest symbolizing connection, intimacy, and balance.

The illustration style uses fine pen-and-ink linework, reinforcing the handcrafted nature of the brand’s products while introducing a whimsical, storytelling element to the identity.

⸻

Identity Principles

Mirrored Symmetry
Interlocking antlers create a symbolic crest representing connection and intimacy.

Hand-Drawn Craft
Detailed linework evokes artisanal production and authenticity.

Dual Colorways
Gold-on-black and terracotta-on-white versions provide versatility across packaging and print.

Mythical Character
The jackalope introduces humor, mythology, and personality into the brand system.

Stationery System

Building a Cohesive Brand World

The stationery system extends the brand’s illustrated personality into tactile printed materials.

Botanical borders, playful fruit illustrations, and cheeky messaging establish a tone that is both sophisticated and mischievous.

Key pieces include:

• illustrated letterhead
• custom envelope system with patterned liners
• thank-you cards included in product shipments

These elements help transform routine communication into part of the brand experience.

⸻

Packaging Design

Designing the Unboxing Experience

A major focus of the thesis explored how packaging design can elevate a product purchase into a memorable brand moment.

The packaging system treats each order as a gift experience, combining illustrated wrapping paper, custom tissue, and playful messaging.

Key packaging elements include:

• the “For Your Peach” gift box
• art-nouveau inspired botanical wrapping paper
• custom jackalope logo seal tape
• illustrated lubricant packaging

The result is packaging designed to feel premium, playful, and collectible.

⸻

Brand Extensions

Merchandise & Lifestyle Products

To test the flexibility of the identity system, the thesis expanded the brand into a series of lifestyle merchandise and promotional items.

These included:

• branded apparel
• condom packaging
• reusable water bottles
• promotional accessories

Each item reinforces the brand’s playful, nature-inspired tone while maintaining visual consistency across the identity system.

⸻

Website Design

Translating the Brand into Digital Space

The thesis also included the design of a fully responsive e-commerce experience.

The website extends the illustrated identity into a digital environment featuring:

• bold illustrated hero sections
• product galleries and category navigation
• brand storytelling pages
• responsive layouts across devices

The digital experience was designed to feel as distinctive and expressive as the physical packaging.

Thesis Outcome

This project demonstrates how thoughtful design can reshape the visual language of industries often constrained by stigma.

By grounding the Sequoia brand in craftsmanship, humor, and natural storytelling, the identity reframes adult wellness products as approachable, beautifully designed consumer goods.
