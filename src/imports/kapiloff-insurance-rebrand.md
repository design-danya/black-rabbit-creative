Kapiloff Insurance

Legacy Brand Modernization for a New Generation

A strategic rebrand designed to increase clarity, trust, and long-term brand usability.

⸻

Project Overview

Kapiloff Insurance came to the project at a pivotal moment — the business was being passed down to a new generation and needed a visual identity that felt current while preserving the credibility the company had built over time.

The goal was not to reinvent the brand, but to refine and strengthen it for the future.

⸻

The Challenge

The existing identity felt dated and lacked the clarity needed for modern applications. Kapiloff needed a system that would:
	•	feel more contemporary and professional
	•	improve readability and recognition
	•	maintain trust in a highly sensitive industry
	•	create consistency across internal and external materials
	•	allow flexible logo usage moving forward

In the insurance space, even small visual shifts can impact perceived credibility — so the solution required precision.

⸻

The Approach

The redesign focused on strategic simplification.

The new identity prioritizes:
	•	clean, confident typography
	•	stronger visual hierarchy
	•	improved scalability
	•	a more modern trust signal

A key move was refining the “K” into a more recognizable standalone mark, giving Kapiloff a flexible brand asset that can work across multiple touchpoints.

The color system was also updated, shifting toward a structured blue palette to better align with industry expectations of trust, stability, and professionalism.

⸻

Brand Identity System

The final deliverable was a fully functional identity system designed for real-world use.

Scope included:
	•	Primary and secondary logo lockups
	•	Standalone “K” mark
	•	Defined typography system
	•	Unified color palette
	•	Email-safe font guidance
	•	Logo usage variations and rules

Every element was designed to help the Kapiloff team maintain consistency across documents, marketing materials, and digital communications.

⸻

Impact

The refreshed identity positions Kapiloff Insurance for its next chapter while preserving the authority of the original brand.

The new system delivers:
	•	stronger visual clarity
	•	improved brand recognition
	•	greater internal consistency
	•	a more modern and approachable presence
