Hendrick’s Lutherie

Heritage-Focused Brand Transformation

A refined identity system designed to elevate a skilled guitar repair shop into a more credible, heritage-driven brand.

⸻

Project Overview

Hendrick’s Lutherie needed a more professional visual identity that reflected the depth of craftsmanship behind the work. The existing logo leaned casual and did not fully communicate the expertise and tradition associated with professional lutherie.

The objective was to develop an identity that felt established, trustworthy, and rooted in traditional craft while remaining clean and versatile for modern applications.

⸻

The Challenge

The original guitar pick logo conveyed creativity but lacked the authority expected from a specialist repair shop.

The new identity needed to:
	•	strengthen professional credibility
	•	reflect traditional craftsmanship
	•	move beyond hobbyist visual cues
	•	improve scalability across applications
	•	create a cohesive, flexible logo system

The brand needed to feel more established without losing its handcrafted character.

⸻

The Approach

The redesign centered on strategic repositioning through form and symbolism.

A circular badge format was developed to evoke heritage craftsmanship and create a stronger visual presence across merchandise, signage, and digital use. At the center, a refined lyre symbol replaced the generic guitar imagery, signaling deeper musical tradition and artisanal expertise.

The visual system emphasizes:
	•	heritage-inspired structure
	•	refined, etched-style typography
	•	warm, wood-influenced color relationships
	•	strong silhouette for recognizability
	•	flexible logo variations for real-world use

⸻

Brand Identity System

The final deliverable was a comprehensive logo system designed for versatility and long-term brand consistency.

Scope included:
	•	Primary circular badge
	•	Horizontal logo configuration
	•	Standalone lyre mark
	•	Alternative refined mark
	•	Typography guidance
	•	Warm, craftsmanship-inspired color palette

Each variation was designed to maintain clarity and brand recognition across both large and small applications.

⸻

Before and After

The transformation shifted the brand from a casual, hobbyist impression to a more established and credible presence.

Before:
An orange guitar pick mark that felt creative but informal.

After:
A structured heritage badge that communicates craftsmanship, longevity, and professional expertise.

⸻

Impact

The refreshed identity positions Hendrick’s Lutherie as a more credible and established repair specialist within the musical instrument space.

The new system delivers:
	•	stronger professional presence
	•	improved brand recognition
	•	greater application flexibility
	•	clearer heritage positioning
